## Open Questions

- **Art assets** — Section 4 (Create Your Character) is the one remaining art-dependent section. A character portrait or race selection illustration would make it sing, but the compact inline table holds it together in the meantime.
- **The Four Founders** — Seraphine, Aldric, Thornwen, and Varek are strong enough characters to earn their own visual treatment. If you ever build out lore pages, a 4-column founder grid (portrait + name + epithet) would be a natural extension of the grid pattern.
- **Description field** — Based on this content, a candidate meta description would be: *"Veils of Fate: Tales of Eldermoor is a free browser-based choose-your-own-adventure RPG. Build your character, explore branching storylines, and uncover the mystery of the Amethyst Veil."*
