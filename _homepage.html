The Hidden Truth

Eldermoor Awaits

Three hundred years ago, four founders built a city and placed a gem at its heart. The histories call them heroes. The gem knows better.

The Purple Gem

At the heart of Eldermoor pulses a crystal older than the city itself. It was created to preserve truth — and truth is exactly what it holds. The gem remembers every secret, every betrayal, every soul bound within it. Now its light is fading, and the children born of its glow are the only ones who can learn why.

The Founders' Lie

Four founders. Three martyrs. One monster. The stories say they gave their lives for Eldermoor. The truth is that one of them took the others — binding their souls to fuel centuries of stolen immortality. The hero the city still celebrates is the very thing that may destroy it.

Forge Your Fate

Forge alliances or walk alone. Show mercy or deliver destruction. The path through Eldermoor branches at every turn — different allies, different truths, different consequences. Four endings await, and the choices you make from the very first scene determine which one you'll earn.
